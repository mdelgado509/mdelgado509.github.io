Michael Delgado

Software Engineer - Musician

About

Michael Delgado is a full-stack developer from the Greater New York Area working in Javascript, Python, HTML, CSS, Express, React, Node and whatever other technologies the job calls for. Before making the transition to tech, he was working as a professional musician in New York City, performing with Rock & Roll Hall of Fame inductees, touring and recording in Sydney, Australia, and teaching young aspiring musicians at a non-profit music education program. He is a proud graduate of Princeton University, General Assembly's Software Engineering Immersive, and has even spent time at Oxford University conducting independent research. His abiity to learn quickly, adapt to new environments, and work autonomously make him a great candidate for any team looking for a new collaborator.
